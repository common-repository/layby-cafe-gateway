This is the official WooCommerce extension to receive payments using the South African Layby Cafe payments provider.

== Description ==

The Layby Cafe extension for WooCommerce enables you to accept payments via layby for a month to month payment Deposits via one of South Africaâ€™s EFT.

= Why choose LaybyCafe? =

Layby Cafe gives your customers more flexibility including putting down deposits, ordering ahead of time or paying on a weekly, monthly or annual basis.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this require a Layby Cafe merchant account? =

Yes! A Layby Cafe merchant account, merchant token is required for this gateway to function.

= Does this require an SSL certificate? =

An SSL certificate is recommended for additional safety and security for your customers.

= Where can I find register? =

You can create your merchant account by visiting(https://mbeta.laybycafe.com/signup)

= Where can I get support or talk to other users? =

If you get stuck, you can ask for help by emailing helpdesk@laybycafe.co.za.
